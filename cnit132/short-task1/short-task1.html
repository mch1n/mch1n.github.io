We are a global ecommerce company with over 100 locations worldwide. We sell collector coins.

Coin collecting, one of the world’s oldest hobbies, was once practiced exclusively by kings, queens, and the very wealthy. Today, anyone can be a coin collector and own a piece of history from My Cool Coin Company. Our wide selection of numismatic items include gold, silver, and platinum coins, program coins, annual coin sets, proof sets, commemorative coins, and uncirculated coins.

Whether seeking a stunning foundation piece to begin a new collection or looking for a one-of-a-kind gift for coin enthusiasts, My Cool Coin Company’s best sellers are a wonderful place to start. Discover keepsakes beloved by collectors around the world for their beauty and symbolic representation of our Nation’s history and values.
